Stem is where curious people collect and share the things they find interesting.

Create a stem for any topic you care about. Architecture, fermentation, film theory, urbanism, whatever you're into. Save articles, videos, papers, and links as you discover them. Others can follow your stems and discover what you're exploring.

Think of it as what Goodreads did for books and Letterboxd did for movies, but for everything else. Your niche interests, organized and shareable.

COLLECT
Save links from anywhere. Articles, YouTube videos, tweets, research papers, recipes. Use the Share Extension to save directly from Safari or any app in two taps.

CURATE
Organize your finds into stems, each one a collection around a topic. Add notes to explain why something is worth reading. Invite others to contribute and it becomes a branch.

DISCOVER
Follow people whose taste you trust. Browse by category. See what others are exploring in areas you care about. Your feed shows you the best finds from the people and stems you follow.

SHARE
Every stem has its own page on stem.md. Share your collections with anyone, even if they don't have the app.

No algorithms deciding what you see. No ads. No news. No politics. Just people collecting and sharing the things that make them curious.

If you can't eat your lunch without watching a random nerdy video on YouTube, Stem is for you.
